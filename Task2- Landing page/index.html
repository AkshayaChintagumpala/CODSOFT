About South Korea

South Korea is a dynamic country where rich traditions meet advanced technology. Famous for K-pop, K-beauty, and stunning travel destinations, it captivates global audiences. Balancing heritage and modern growth, South Korea remains a top cultural and travel hotspot.


Quick Facts

  • Capital: Seoul
  • Population: ~52 million
  • Language: Korean
  • Currency: South Korean Won (₩)

Korean Culture & Trends

K-pop

K-pop is a global sensation, led by groups like BTS and BLACKPINK.

K-beauty

K-beauty focuses on skincare innovation for glowing skin.

Traditional Hanbok

Hanbok is Korea’s traditional dress for festivals and ceremonies.

Korean Festivals

Festivals like Chuseok celebrate Korean culture with food and rituals.

Flavors of Korea

Kimchi

Spicy fermented cabbage, a staple in Korean cuisine.

Bibimbap

Mixed rice with vegetables, beef, and egg, served with spicy sauce.

Tteokbokki

Chewy rice cakes in sweet and spicy sauce, a favorite street food.

Bulgogi

Marinated grilled beef with a savory-sweet flavor, popular worldwide.

Top Travel Destinations

  • Seoul: Where tradition meets modern life
  • Jeju Island:Nature’s paradise of waterfalls and beaches.
  • Busan: Sun, seafood, and vibrant city life
